Characters

  • Jaison / Minnal Murali: A carefree tailor who gains superpowers after being struck by lightning. He initially enjoys his abilities for fun but is later forced to embrace his role as a hero when his village is threatened.
  • Shibu / The Villain: A troubled man who also gains powers from the same lightning strike. Unlike Jaison, he uses his abilities for revenge, becoming the biggest threat to the town.
  • Bincy: A martial arts instructor who supports Jaison in his journey, helping him train and understand his responsibilities as a superhero.
  • Dasan: Jaison’s best friend, providing comic relief and unknowingly pushing Jaison toward realizing his heroic potential.
  • SI Sajan: A police officer investigating the strange events in the village, initially skeptical but later realizing the truth about the superhuman conflict.
  • Josemon: Jaison’s uncle, who offers him wisdom and emotional support, unknowingly guiding him toward heroism.
  • Sophie: A journalist investigating the mysterious occurrences in the village, slowly uncovering Jaison’s secret identity.
  • Ravi: A businessman with hidden ties to Selvan, exploiting the chaos for his own gain.
  • Master Krishnan: A retired scientist who helps Jaison understand his powers and the science behind the lightning strike.
