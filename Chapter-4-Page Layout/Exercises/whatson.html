1. Discover Digital: Participation and Inclusion

Speaker: Maisie Peebles, Discover Digital Development Officer

Overview: This session explores the next steps for the Discover Digital project, which is focused on making digital health and care more inclusive and accessible. Join Maisie to learn about the ongoing developments and how you can participate in the project.

2. Better Futures: Harnessing Digital Technology to Tackle Homelessness

Overview: This session highlights the Better Futures project, which leverages digital solutions to support collaborative working in tackling homelessness. The session includes insights from Scottish Government staff and Simon Scotland, followed by a live demonstration of the system.

3. Realising Value from Digital Investment

Overview: This Derek Hoy Memorial Lecture will focus on realising the value of digital investments in healthcare and social services. Andy Kinnear will discuss how we can ensure that investments in digital tools lead to improved outcomes for clinicians and patients.

4. Sustainability in Housing: The Intersection of Health, Heat, and Housing

Overview: Explore how sustainable housing improves health outcomes. This session will examine the relationship between housing, heat, and health, and the role of IoT and environmental sensors in managing these elements.

Speakers:

  • Stephanie Elliot, Technology Enabled Care Lead, Scottish Federation of Housing Associations
  • Cassandra Dove, Policy Lead, SFHA
  • John Griffiths, CTO and Director at Safehouse Technologies

5. Back to The Future: Your Path to Digital Skills and Leadership

Overview: This interactive session will help you take the lead on your own digital skills journey. The session will introduce the Building Digital Skills & Leadership Programme, a resource designed to support individuals in the health and care sector in enhancing their digital capabilities.

Speakers:

  • Paula Baird, Programme Lead, Digitally Enabled Workforce, NES
  • Alan Milbourne, Policy Manager, Digital Health and Care Workforce, Scottish Government
