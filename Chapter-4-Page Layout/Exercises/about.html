ABOUT

What is Digi Fest?

  • Digi Fest is an annual celebration of digital technology, innovation, and collaboration in Cardiff.
  • The event showcases the latest trends in digital technology and highlights the role of collaboration in the tech industry.

History of Digi Fest

  • Started as a small event, Digi Fest has grown into a prominent gathering for tech enthusiasts and professionals in Cardiff.
  • Each year, it brings together industry leaders, startups, and creatives to celebrate digital innovation.
  • Notable past events have included ground-breaking discussions and memorable speakers.

Theme for the Year

  • This year’s theme is "collaboration," focusing on how working together can drive innovation in the digital world.
  • The event will highlight various collaborative projects happening in Cardiff’s tech ecosystem.
  • Collaboration is key in the digital age, and this year's event will explore how to leverage it to create impactful solutions.

What to Expect

  • Keynote speakers, workshops, panel discussions, and networking opportunities designed to inspire and educate attendees.
  • Sessions will cover the latest trends in digital tech, innovation, and collaboration strategies.
  • The event is ideal for professionals, students, entrepreneurs, and anyone passionate about digital technology.

Collaborations Highlighted

  • Explore the latest collaborations between tech companies, startups, and academic institutions in Cardiff.
  • See how local businesses are working together to drive innovation and contribute to Cardiff's growing reputation as a digital hub.

Cardiff as a Digital Hub

  • Cardiff is emerging as a vibrant center for digital technology and innovation.
  • Local tech companies, digital agencies, and universities are leading the way in advancing digital capabilities.
  • The city’s thriving digital scene is a key focus of Digi Fest, which highlights the local contributions to the global tech landscape.

Schedule/Agenda Overview

Past Highlights & Testimonials
